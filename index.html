SERVICES

Residential electrical services

Complete electrical wiring of new single family homes, Town homes, Apartments and Condominiums. Renovations, Additions, Unfinished basements, New kitchens, Service upgrades, Recessed lights, Ceiling fans, Roof fans, Trouble shooting, Installation of new service panels, sub panels, Replacement of all electrical devices and light fixtures, New circuits for appliances and service calls.

Commercial electrical services

Complete electrical wiring of new buildings. Renovations, Additions, Tenant fit out, Office renovations, New restaurants, Stores, Warehouses, Hotels, Motels, Trouble shooting, Building electrical demolition, installation of new lighting systems, Retrofit and maintenance of exterior lighting systems, Power distribution and new branch circuit installation.

Interior lighting systems

Updating current interior and exterior lighting to a more energy efficient and modern system. New installation of lighting systems in office buildings, Schools, Stores, Restaurants,Warehouses, Hotels, Motels and apartment complexes.
